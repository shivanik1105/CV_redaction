Senior Automotive Embedded Software Engineer

MANDATORY REQUIREMENTS (Must Have):
• 5+ years of automotive embedded software development experience
• Expert-level proficiency in Embedded C and C++
• Strong AUTOSAR architecture and integration experience
• Experience with Infineon Aurix or similar automotive microcontrollers
• ISO 26262 functional safety knowledge and application
• Automotive SPICE process experience
• CAN/CAN-FD protocol expertise

NICE TO HAVE (Preferred):
• ISO/SAE 21434 automotive cybersecurity certification
• Electric vehicle or hybrid powertrain experience
• Battery management system (BMS) development
• Vector CANoe, CANalyzer tools
• Requirements engineering with DOORS, PTC Integrity, or CodeBeamer
• Experience with major OEMs (Mercedes, BMW, Volkswagen, etc.)
• Team leadership or technical architect experience
• MISRA C/C++ compliance expertise

ROLE DESCRIPTION:
We are seeking a senior embedded software engineer to lead AUTOSAR architecture design and integration for next-generation electric vehicle platforms. The ideal candidate will work with cross-functional teams to develop safety-critical automotive systems, conduct SPICE assessments, and mentor junior engineers.

Key responsibilities include:
- Designing and implementing AUTOSAR software components
- Conducting requirements engineering for electric powertrain systems
- Performing functional safety analysis per ISO 26262
- Integrating software on Infineon Aurix multicore platforms
- Leading technical reviews and SPICE assessments
- Collaborating with international OEM customers

This role requires strong technical expertise, excellent communication skills, and the ability to work independently in a fast-paced automotive development environment.
