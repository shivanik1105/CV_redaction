# Recruiter Quick Reference - Human Review Workflow

## 🎯 NO AUTO-REJECT POLICY

**AI NEVER REJECTS CANDIDATES**  
Only humans make rejection decisions. AI provides recommendations only.

---

## 📊 Understanding AI Verdicts

| Verdict | Meaning | Your Action |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| **SHORTLIST** | ✅ Strong match (≥80% requirements) | Review as priority candidate |
| **BACKUP** | ⚡ Decent match (60-79% requirements) | Review if shortlist exhausted |
| **REVIEW** | ⚠️ AI uncertain or low match | **HUMAN REVIEW REQUIRED** |

### Confidence Score Guide

- **90-100%**: AI very confident in verdict
- **70-89%**: AI confident, review top 50
- **<70%**: 🔴 **MANDATORY HUMAN REVIEW** - AI not confident enough

---

## 🔴 Human Review Queue

### When Candidates Require Review:
1. **Low AI Confidence (<70%)**
   - AI verdict uncertain due to:
     - Vague CV descriptions
     - Incomplete experience details
     - Unclear skill levels
     - Borderline match score

2. **Always Review Top 50**
   - Even strong AI verdicts need human validation
   - Check for cultural fit, soft skills, potential

## 🖥️ Dashboard Workflow

### Step 1: Check Review Queue
```
Dashboard → "⚠️ Human Review Required" Panel
         → Click "📋 Load Review Queue"
```

**You'll see:**
- Candidates with confidence <70%
- Warning badge: "⚠️ LOW CONFIDENCE: XX%"
- AI verdict and reasoning

### Step 2: Review Candidate
**Check:**
- ✓ Core technical skills match
- ✓ Years of experience appropriate
- ✓ Domain relevance
- ✓ Leadership indicators (if needed)
- ✓ Education requirements
- ✓ Verdict reasoning makes sense

**Read:**
- Cleaned narrative summary
- Verdict reason (AI explanation)
- Matched vs. missing requirements

### Step 3: Make Your Decision

**Three Override Options:**

| Button | When to Use |
|--------|-------------|
| **✓ SHORTLIST** | Candidate meets requirements despite AI uncertainty. Proceed to interview. |
| **✗ REJECT** | Candidate does not meet core requirements. Clear gaps identified. |
| **⏸ ON HOLD** | Need more information. Follow up with phone screen or clarification. |

### Step 4: Document Decision
1. Add notes explaining your reasoning
2. Enter your recruiter ID
3. Click confirm

**Your override is permanently recorded in audit trail.**

## 🔍 Interpreting AI Verdicts

### What to Look For:

**1. Confidence Score < 70%**
- 🚩 Vague CV language
- 🚩 Incomplete work history
- 🚩 Unclear project details
- 🚩 AI cannot verify claims

**2. Match Score vs. Verdict Mismatch**
- Match 80% but verdict REVIEW → Check reasoning
- Likely: Critical skill missing despite high match

**3. Verdict Reason Quality**
- Good: "7 yrs Python/AWS, led 3 microservice projects. Missing K8s."
- Bad: "Good skills and experience" (shouldn't happen)

## ⚠️ Required Actions

### You MUST Review:
1. ✅ All candidates in Human Review Queue (confidence <70%)
2. ✅ Top 50 candidates regardless of AI verdict
3. ✅ Any candidate before final rejection
4. ✅ Borderline cases (match score 60-79%)

### You SHOULD Review:
- Candidates with leadership requirements (AI can't assess soft skills)
- Cultural fit questions (AI doesn't know company culture)
- High-potential junior candidates (AI focuses on experience)
- Non-traditional backgrounds (career changers, bootcamp grads)

## 📞 Need Help?

### Common Questions:

**Q: Can I trust AI SHORTLIST verdicts?**  
A: Check confidence score. If ≥80%, AI is confident. Still review top 50.

**Q: What if I disagree with AI?**  
A: Override it! Your decision is final. Add notes explaining why.

**Q: How to handle "REVIEW" verdicts?**  
A: Read CV yourself, check reasoning, make human judgment call.

**Q: Can I reject without reviewing?**  
A: NO. System requires human review for all rejections (ethical AI).
